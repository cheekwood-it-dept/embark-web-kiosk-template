How do I use Zoomify?
%{[Objects_1]Disp_Title}%
(Click to return to Description)

Use alt(option)-click-drag to do a zoom rectangle.
The x, y, and z coordinates of the zoomed image are saved in the URL. This allows browser bookmarking to the exact detail of the image you wish to highlight.
